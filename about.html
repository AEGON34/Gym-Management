ABOUT US

A role-based Gym Management System built for structured operations and measurable progress.

Project Overview

PowerFit Gym Management System is a university software engineering project designed to streamline day-to-day gym operations. Its goal is to reduce manual paperwork and provide reliable, consistent information across the gym. The platform centralizes member records, trainer profiles, workout routines, diet plans, and health progress in a clear, structured interface. Role-based access ensures each user sees the tools they need: Admins manage registrations, memberships, reports, and statistics; Trainers create and assign workouts and nutrition plans while monitoring progress; Members view their schedules, track results, and stay consistent with guidance. Progress tracking and printable workout and diet plans support both in-gym and at-home use. Built-in reporting helps identify trends such as attendance and performance over time. The system also improves communication between trainers and members through dedicated pages, encouraging timely feedback and better outcomes.
